Mythicon Co

Build up your own imagination
Mythicon Co. is a growing company which creates technology- based toys with the aim of teaching kids to code while bringing fun to their playtime. By taking the form of mythical creatures such as Unicorns, Dragons etc., the Mythicon robot toy allows kids to control their robots movements, lights & sounds by entering different codes through the Mythicode app.
